115最后家庭成员签证为永久签证。申请人在申请或获签该签证时必须在澳大利亚境外。 > The Remaining Relative visa (subclass 115) is a permanent visa. Your application must be made outside Australia and you must be outside Australia when the visa is granted.

1. 针对人群（Who could get this visa）

生活在澳洲境外且唯一的近亲属在澳洲居住的人群

2. 申请条件（Application Requirement）

  • 申请人的唯一近亲属或配偶居住澳大利亚
  • 申请人及随行人员有合法近亲属或者近亲属配偶担保
  • 申请人及随行人员满足健康及品质要求
  • 申请人能够找到为其提供支持的保证人 > you have someone who will provide an assurance of support. A person or organisation must give you an assurance of support. They can be your sponsor or another person. Up to three people can provide an assurance of support for you. > > An assurance of support is a legal commitment to provide you with financial support so you do not have to rely on social security payments.

保证人需：

  • 为年满18岁的申请人缴纳保证金：
    • AUD5000-主申
    • AUD2000-其他成年随行人员
  • 偿还在澳大利亚居住前两年所领取的可收回社会保险费用

The bond is refunded two years after your first entry to Australia once your visa has been granted. Any debts to the government are deducted from the bond and only the balance is refunded.

不能申请该签证的情况：

  • 申请人或其配偶有其他居住在澳洲境外的近亲属，或者申请人的近亲属持临时签证、过桥签证或非法居住在澳洲
  • 申请人的担保人或其配偶持有最后家庭成员签证（835/115）、优惠家庭签证（104）或家庭签证（806）或为他人担保过该类签证

可随行人员:

  • 申请人配偶
  • 申请人或其配偶的受抚养子女

3. 申请流程（Application Process）

基本表格：

  • Form 47OF Application for migration to Australia by other family members
  • Form 47A Details of Child or Other Dependent Family Member Aged 18 Years or Over（如果适用）
  • Form 1229 Consent form to grant an Australian visa to a child under the age of 18 years（如果适用）
  • Form 40 Sponsorship for migration to Australia

其他材料： 申请人的身份证明、关系证明、子女、健康及无犯罪材料

申请提交： > Lodge your application at one of the immigration offices outside Australia

4. 费用（Cost）

基本申请费用：Base application charge($3870)+Addirtional applicant charge 18 and over($1935)+Addirtional applicant charge under 18($970) 其他费用：公证、体检等费用
